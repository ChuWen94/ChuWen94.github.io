Residential electricity consumption

Estimates for Rhineland-Palatinate, 2011

To evaluate the use of primary data in estimating private household electricity consumption at high regional resolution a synthetic population was generated for each municipality (Gemeinde) in Rhineland-Palatinate via Iterative Proportional Fitting (IPF) for the year 2011. Estimates are based on data on household and dwelling characteristics from the 2011 German Census and primary data on private household electricity consumption from the German Residential Energy Consumption Survey (GRECS).

Methodology to be published under "A survey-based approach to estimate residential electricity consumption at a German municipal level " as part of the Working Papers Sustainability and Innovation of the Fraunhofer Institute for Systems and Innovation Research ISI.
